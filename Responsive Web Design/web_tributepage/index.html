Daedric Prince:Mephala

Mephala is one of the Daedric Princes which revolves around lies and deciet.This daedric prince is also known as the WebSpinner.

Mephala is worshipped by the Dunmer and also a part of their clan system.The Morag Tong are a one such prominent group of assassins that worship Mephala.

Mephala also forged the Ebony Blade ,which grows stronger and stronger as one sheds the blood of those who trust him the most.
This blade also drives the user crazy as one continues to use it.

Mephala and Hermaus Mora are said to be siblings in the Elder Scrolls Lore
